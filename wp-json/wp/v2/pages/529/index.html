Welcome to TazPin, the premier pinball event of the year!<\/p>\n\n\n\n

TazPin is an annual event that celebrates the rich history and exciting future of pinball machines. This year, TazPin 2024 will be held on the weekend of August 3 \u2013 4, and will be the third year running after its successful inaugural year in 2022. The event is put on by dedicated volunteers who are passionate about pinball and want to share their love of the game with others.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

At TazPin, attendees will have the opportunity to play over 50 pinball machines from both classic and modern eras, thanks to the generous contributions of private collectors who have opened up their collections for the event. Whether you are a seasoned pinball pro or a newcomer to the game, TazPin has something for everyone.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Not only will you be able to play your favorite machines, but you\u2019ll also have the chance to meet and connect with fellow pinball enthusiasts. TazPin is a welcoming and inclusive community that values camaraderie and shared experiences. You\u2019ll be able to swap stories, trade tips, and maybe even make some new friends!<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Whether you\u2019re a die-hard pinball fan or just looking for a fun weekend activity, TazPin is the place to be. Don\u2019t miss out on this amazing opportunity to experience the thrill of pinball and connect with others who share your passion. We can\u2019t wait to see you at TazPin!<\/p>\n\n\n
